Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado (竈門かまど 炭たん治じ郎ろう Kamado Tanjirō?) is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado, back into a human and to hunt down and kill demons, and later swore to defeat Muzan Kibutsuji, the King of Demons, in order to prevent others from suffering the same fate as him.

Before he became a Demon Slayer, Tanjiro was a coal burner before his family was slaughtered by Muzan, while his younger sister, Nezuko, was turned into a demon.

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke Hashibira (嘴平伊之助) is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and one of the traveling companions of Tanjiro Kamado and a main protagonist in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) series. He was discovered to be the fifth winner of the Final Selection, and the first to make it up and down Fujikasane Mountain before the others. Inosuke has a very short temper and leaps into fights without hesitation, even attacking the box Nezuko hides inside after detecting a Demon inside. In the anime, during the Natagumo Arc, he has a big role in the fight against the Father Spider Demon. He likes to think that he is the best warrior in any situation, confronting most people he meets which tend to lead him into unnecessary danger.

Kanao Tsuyuri

Kanao Tsuyuri (栗花落カナヲ) is a talented young female
Demon Slayer in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) series.
One of the five to survive the final selection, she was first introduced
as a quiet young girl gazing at a butterfly. She is the adoptive sister
of Kanae Kocho and Shinobu Kocho, the former tsuguko of the latter sister.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma (我妻善逸) is usually seen acting like a coward, he has low esteem and sometimes gives up when given a job. He is a Demon Slayer member of the Demon Slayer Corps, one of the main traveling companions of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the main protagonists in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) series. He faces the Tongue Demon defeating him while he is asleep.

Murata

Murata is a low-ranking Demon Slayer that first made an appearance
in the Natagumo Mountain Arc in the manga Chapter 28 and anime Episode
15. He is voiced in Japanese by Koki Miyata. His features are bland,
unremarkable, with black eyes and straight black hair split in the centre.
It is mentioned that his family was killed by Demons before he became a
Demon Slayer. Murata is a Total Concentration water breathing user but not
to the same degree as Tanjiro. Helping Tanjiro and Inosuke, he struggles
with his manipulated comrades, demonstrating his courage in buying time.

MEET

Genya Shinazugawa

Genya Shinazugawa (不死川 玄弥) is one of the Five survivors of the
Final Selection and is a Demon Slayer in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon
Slayer) series. He is the younger brother to the Wind Pillar, named
Sanemi Shinazugawa. His circumstance as a Demon Slayer is special
in the sense that he has no talent for Breath Styles, but is still
able to fight against Demons. His Japanese voice actor is Nobuhiko
Okamoto, and in English voiced by Zeno Robinson. His first Manga
debut is in Chapter 6, and Episode 4 in the anime series.

Ozaki

Ozaki first makes an appearance in the Natagumo Mountain Arc
in Chapter 30 of the manga, and Episode 16 of the anime
series. Voiced in Japanese by Hisako Tojo. Ozaki is a thin,
brown-eyed young girl wearing her hair in a medium-length
ponytail. She is shown to be considerate and selfless, warning
fellow Demon Slayers when she was being manipulated not to
touch her. Tanjiro, hesitating for a moment, thought of the
safest way of immobilizing Ozaki without hurting her. She is
a low-ranking Demon Slayer, but has the ability to deal with
the demons easily.

Aoi Kanzaki

Aoi Kanzaki (神崎 アオイ) is a Demon Slayer that works
as a helper at the butterfly estate rather than the
battlefield. She's got deep blue eyes matching her two
blue butterfly clips that hold her twintail hair in place.
She taught Tanjiro, Zenitsu and Inosuke, establishing a
demanding regime that often forced them to the brink. She
has a strong demeanor resembling a younger Shinobu Kocho,
always looking furious. While she often shows sensitive
feelings including bursting into tears while thinking about
Inosuke's injury demonstrating that she is kind and concerned
emotionally. She first makes an appearance in the Functional
Recovery Training Arc in Chapter 48 of the manga, and Episode
23 of the anime series voiced by Yuri Ehara.

Sumi Nakahara

Sumi Nakahara is a petite girl with light skin, button-like
eyes, wearing her hair in low twintails fastened with blue
butterfly clips. She is a servant of the butterfly estate,
after having her family killed she was taken in. Appearing in
the Functional Recovery Training Arc in Chapter 49 of the manga,
and Episode 24 of the anime series voiced by Ayumi Mano.

MEET

Kiyo Terauchi

Kiyo Terauchi is a servant of the Butterfly Estate.
She was drawn in by Demons after having killed her
father. Kiyo is a teenager with light skin and a pair
of black eyes, with shoulder length black hair wrapped
on each side of her head with two pink butterfly clips.
Wearing a long white dress for a nurse, and a pink bow
around her waist. She makes her debut in Chapter 48 of
the manga, and Episode 23 of the anime.
