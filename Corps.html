CORPS
The Ubuyashiki Family is an ancient and noble family tasked with leading and organizing the Demon Slayer Corps in their quest to exterminate demons and defend humanity.
The Hashira are the highest-ranking Demon Slayers in the Demon Slayer Corps.
Demon Slayers are members of the Demon Slayer Corps whose main job is to hunt and slay demons.
The Butterfly Mansion is the manor that the late Flower Hashira Kanae Kocho, Insect Hashira Shinobu Kocho, Tsuguko Kanao Tsuyuri, and assistants Aoi Kanzaki, Sumi Nakahara, Kiyo Terauchi, and Naho Takada live in. The manor is often used as a recovery base for injured Demon Slayers.
Swordsmiths are a dedicated group of highly skilled artisans living and working in the Swordsmith Village.
